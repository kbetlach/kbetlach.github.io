About Me

I was really drawn to computer coding because of its ever evolving nature, and how much there is to learn. I hope that over time my portfolio will be ever evolving as well, reflecting the many steps I will be taking in this new pursuit. What I hope you'll find in my portfolio is my dedication to creativity, as well as my passion and drive and constant desire to improve.
