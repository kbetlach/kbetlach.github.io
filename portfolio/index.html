About Me

My name is Kyle Betlach, and I'm a certified MERN stack web developer. In addition to my University of Minnesota coding bootcamp certificate, I have bachelor's degrees in anthropology and religious studies from Hamline Unversity. I have spent my working life bouncing around between the natural foods industry, archaeology, and board game distribution, but decided to change direction and pursue full stack development. I chose to get into coding as a way to pursue my interest in technology, in addition to having an outlet to flex my creative muscle.

In my freetime, my hobbies include tabletop board gaming, watching the Minnesota United soccer team, listening to music, and spending time with friends and my girlfriend Ashley. I'm a lifelong Minnesotan, and a proud resident of Saint Paul.

I was really drawn to computer coding because of its ever-evolving nature, and how much there is to learn. What I hope you'll find in my portfolio is my dedication to creativity, as well as my passion, drive, and constant desire to improve.
